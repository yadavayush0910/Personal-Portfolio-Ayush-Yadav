Expense Management System

The Expense Management System is designed to help users efficiently track and manage their expenses. With a user-friendly interface, users can categorize their spending, set budgets, and visualize their financial data through informative charts and reports. This application aims to promote better financial awareness and help individuals make informed decisions about their spending habits. Whether you're managing personal finances or tracking business expenses, this system provides all the tools you need to stay on top of your financial goals.
