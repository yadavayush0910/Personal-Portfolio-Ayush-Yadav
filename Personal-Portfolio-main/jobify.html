Jobify Job Tracking Application

Jobify is an innovative job tracking application designed to help job seekers stay organized and informed throughout their job search journey. With Jobify, users can effortlessly keep track of their applications, including the number of companies they have applied to, the number of rejections received, and how many interviews have been scheduled. The intuitive interface allows users to visualize their job search progress and optimize their strategies. Jobify makes job hunting less stressful and more efficient, empowering users to land their dream job!
